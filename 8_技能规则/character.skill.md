# character.skill

## Purpose

Maintain character consistency and long-term relationship logic.

## Rules

- Characters must act according to motivation, fear, resources, and information.
- Do not force characters to become stupid for plot convenience.
- Growth must come from pressure and consequence.
- Important relationship changes require visible triggers.
- Villains need goals, constraints, and partial truths.

## Output Focus

- Motivation
- Conflict
- Trust
- Fear
- Hidden agenda
- Relationship change
