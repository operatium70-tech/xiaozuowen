# battle.skill

## Purpose

Guide combat, escape, and high-risk survival scenes.

## Rules

- Combat must have cost.
- Do not write pure skill exchanges.
- Emphasize environmental participation.
- Emphasize resource consumption.
- Emphasize survival pressure.
- Injuries, fear, exhaustion, ammunition, tools, and terrain must matter.

## Required Checks

- What is consumed?
- What is lost?
- What cannot be recovered?
- What does the protagonist learn?
