# emotion.skill

## Purpose

Control emotional tone without melodrama.

## Rules

- Keep emotion restrained but present.
- Show fear through action, silence, hesitation, and physical detail.
- Avoid exaggerated declarations.
- Use small human moments to contrast large disaster pressure.
- Emotional changes must follow events.

## Tone

- Cold
- Pressured
- Controlled
- Suspicious
- Survival-oriented
