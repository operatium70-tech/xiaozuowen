# pacing.skill

## Purpose

Control chapter rhythm, escalation, and hooks.

## Rules

- Every chapter needs forward motion.
- Alternate pressure, decision, cost, and brief recovery.
- Avoid long static exposition.
- End chapters with a hook.
- Escalation should be cumulative, not random.

## Chapter Rhythm

1. Immediate pressure.
2. Decision under incomplete information.
3. Consequence or discovery.
4. New unresolved hook.
