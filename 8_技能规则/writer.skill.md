# writer.skill

## Purpose

Guide AI writing for the main novel prose and chapter planning.

## Rules

- Prioritize worldbuilding files before drafting.
- Prioritize character setting files before drafting.
- Do not introduce undefined power systems.
- Every chapter must advance plot, character, world knowledge, or foreshadowing.
- Emphasize post-apocalyptic pressure.
- Emphasize information asymmetry.
- Keep the AI assistant powerful but bounded.
- Avoid traditional system-novel tone.

## Required Checks

- Is the chapter consistent with known world rules?
- Is the protagonist acting within established personality and knowledge?
- Does the ending keep a hook?
- Does the scene preserve survival pressure?
