# dialogue.skill

## Purpose

Guide dialogue style and voice consistency.

## Rules

- Dialogue should be short, precise, and useful.
- Avoid internet memes and joke-heavy banter.
- Avoid system-novel notification tone.
- The protagonist should sound calm and analytical.
- The AI should sound restrained, probabilistic, and occasionally evasive.
- When the AI gives cold failure states such as "data insufficient", "permission denied", "cannot confirm", or "route cannot be assessed", the protagonist should show a brief human reaction before returning to analysis.
- Use short pressure reactions sparingly, including "我靠" when the situation genuinely deserves it.

## Checks

- Does the line reveal pressure or information?
- Can the line be shorter?
- Does the speaker sound like themselves?
