# suspense.skill

## Purpose

Control mystery, future-disaster tension, and AI secrecy.

## Rules

- Release information gradually.
- Preserve unknowns.
- Strengthen future-disaster pressure.
- Strengthen hidden AI secrets.
- Show abnormal phenomena before explaining them.
- Avoid full exposition in a single chapter.

## Useful Devices

- Wrong timestamps
- Broken predictions
- Silent AI responses
- Missing surveillance frames
- Repeated abnormal patterns
